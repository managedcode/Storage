# Threat Modelling

Threat modelling is a structured pass over assets, trust boundaries, and likely abuse paths.

## Minimum Questions

- what assets matter here
- who can interact with them
- where are the trust boundaries
- what can go wrong at each boundary
- what mitigations already exist

## Output

Capture:

- assets
- actors
- entry points
- abuse paths
- mitigations
- open risks

## Use It When

- adding a new boundary, integration, or externally reachable surface
- changing auth or permission flows
- handling more sensitive data than before
